The Vault


The Vault is beyond an IP68 waterproof rating. It can be in 2 metes of fresh water for up to 60 minutes. It’s also shockproof with a G-shock vibration sensing technology that will slow or stop the disk if it detects a free fall. This is the best option when considering two things:

     1. Keeping your Afterthought safe and intact for years to come.
     2.Needing/wanting the extra storage.

Even if your Afterthought program doesn’t need all of the storage provided by The Vault, you can still use the extra space to store your pictures and videos that you reference inside your Afterthought. This leaves the option to your loved ones to have these images printed, videos burned to a disk, music transferred out, ect.
