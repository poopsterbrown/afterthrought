Afterthought Kit

This is the setup of your Afterthought application which comes with one photoshopped start screen, three memory banks, and as many intro videos as desired. The application is custom made around you in every way. It will also include an intro video explaining what this is to your users, and will be stored on a new portable hard drive to both store and protect the finished program and encrypted developer files that will allow your Afterthought to be updated any time you wish to have additional services added. This hard drive is your finished product that you will get to keep. The price of the starter kit varies depending on the type of hard drive you desire.

Concept Features

These are potential add-ons that are currently in development.
Music Player

This is a feature that is almost ready for deployment. While you can talk about all of your favorite music in a memory bank, this allows your guests to actually listen to your favorite songs. 

This can also be used to store your own podcasts, or even share the ones you love. Any type of audio can be kept here.

Photo Album

This is an album that you can limit who can access it, and even then, you can still control who can see which pictures. 

The photo album can store an unlimited amount of pictures as long as you have the storage to support it. 

Chat Bot

This is still in the very early stages, but this would allow your friends and family type to a computer version of yourself that will respond the way that you would respond. 

Virtual Avatar

Simply a concept at this time, this is the full realization of Afterthought. A digital version of yourself that your users could, in some aspect, talk to. 
The hope is to make it responsive to a serious of questions and to be similar to Google or Amazon's Alexa only it would look and sound like you.
