Feature List

Advanced Login

Your Afterthought can be the same presentation for everyone, or you can make a more tailored experience for each person you intend to give this to. 
For example, you could have a Guest mode that would let anyone see only the things you are comfortable being public, while hiding more intimate moments for your spouse, and or kids. An unlimited amount of logins can be created. Each of these logins can have their own personal level of access.  

Greeting Videos

This is the video that plays immediately after someone logs in. It's of you greeting the user. This can be an explanation of what Afterthought is, telling a joke, or just saying hi. If you have multiple greetings stored they can either play at random with each login, or can be used specifically for each user.
